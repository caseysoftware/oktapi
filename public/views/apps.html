 OIDC to SAML SSO
 Inbound SAML to BGCO
 OIDC to SAML over inbound SAML federation
Oktalane users (authenticated by this portal app) can access a deep link to a Sharepoint document on BGCO over an inbound SAML trust.
Oktalane is the IDP for this OKTAPI portal application. There is an inbound SAML trust established between Oktalane and BGCO, which allows Oktalane users to access certain applications on the BGCO org. In this case, we have allowed Oktalane users to have accounts on BGCO's O365 tenant. In this case, we are sharing a deep link to a Sharepoint document that BGCO and Oktalane users can collaborate on.
